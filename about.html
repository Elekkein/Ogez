To provide quality, holistic secondary education grounded in Christian values that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and a strong moral foundation to serve God and humanity effectively.

Our Journey

50+ Years of Excellence

A proud history of shaping generations of Ugandan leaders.

1975
School Founded

Bishop Ogez High School was established by the Diocese of Ankole with a founding enrolment of 120 students and 12 teachers, committed to Christian-based education in Bushenyi.

1985
Expansion of Facilities

New dormitories, a science laboratory, and a library wing were constructed, transforming the school into a full boarding institution capable of accommodating 400 students.

1993
A-Level Stream Introduced

The school introduced Advanced Level education, offering Science and Arts combinations, enabling students to prepare for university without leaving campus.

2005
National Excellence Award

Bishop Ogez High School received the National Education Excellence Award from the Ministry of Education for outstanding UNEB examination results for three consecutive years.

2015
ICT Centre Opened

A state-of-the-art ICT Centre with 30 computers and internet connectivity was launched, marking the school's entry into the digital age of education.

2026
Brand New ICT Laboratory

Upgraded to a 40-computer laboratory with high-speed fibre internet, smart boards in every classroom, and launched an e-learning platform for students and parents.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision, policy, and interaction at Bishop Ogez High School.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and transparency in all our dealings. Students are taught to act with integrity whether being observed or not.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, sports, arts, and character development. Mediocrity is never acceptable.

Compassion

We care for every student's wellbeing — emotionally, spiritually, and physically — ensuring no one is left behind.

Community

We foster a strong sense of belonging, teamwork, and service to the wider Bushenyi community and Uganda at large.

Faith

Rooted in Christian principles, we nurture a deep sense of faith, respect for all religions, and spiritual growth.

Discipline

We believe that self-discipline is the cornerstone of success. It is instilled through consistent routines, mentorship, and accountability.
