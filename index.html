About Our School

Building the Leaders of Tomorrow

Bishop Ogez High School, nestled in the scenic hills of Bushenyi, Western Uganda, has been a beacon of academic excellence since 1975. Founded on strong Christian values, our school combines rigorous academics with character formation to produce well-rounded graduates.

We offer both Ordinary and Advanced level education with streams in Sciences, Arts, and a robust co-curricular program that develops the whole student — mind, body, and spirit.

Why Choose Us

The Bishop Ogez Advantage

What sets us apart as one of the finest secondary schools in Western Uganda.

Qualified Educators

Our team of experienced and dedicated teachers are committed to academic excellence and personal mentorship.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art laboratories, a well-stocked library, ICT centre, and spacious classrooms.

Discipline & Values

Grounded in Christian values, we cultivate integrity, responsibility, and respect in every student.

Academic Results

Consistently high UNEB pass rates with distinctions in both O-Level and A-Level examinations.

Holistic Development

Sports, clubs, music, drama, and community service develop well-rounded future leaders.

Boarding Excellence

Safe, nurturing boarding facilities with balanced meals, medical care, and pastoral support.
