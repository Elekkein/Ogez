Curriculum

O-Level & A-Level Programs

We offer Uganda's national curriculum at both levels, preparing students for UNEB examinations with excellence.

Ordinary Level (S.1 – S.4)

Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) — UNEB

Sciences

Core sciences forming the foundation for A-Level and university studies.

Mathematics Biology Chemistry Physics
Humanities

Social sciences providing understanding of history, society, and economics.

History Geography Commerce Divinity Economics
Languages & Arts

Building strong communication and expression for global readiness.

English Kiswahili Literature French Fine Art

Advanced Level (S.5 – S.6)

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) — UNEB

Sciences Combination

Designed for students pursuing Medicine, Engineering, and Science degrees.

Physics Chemistry Biology Mathematics Subsidiary ICT
Arts & Business

For students heading into Law, Social Sciences, Business, and Public Administration.

Economics History Geography Entrepreneurship Subsidiary Math
Arts Combination

For students pursuing Teaching, Journalism, Literature, and Theology.

Literature in English Divinity History French

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Our campus is equipped with modern infrastructure designed to support comprehensive learning and discovery.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs with modern apparatus for practical learning.

ICT Laboratory

40-computer lab with high-speed fibre internet, used for ICT lessons, research, and e-learning.

School Library

Over 8,000 titles across all subjects, with a dedicated reading room and digital resources.

Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms with smart boards enabling interactive, engaging lessons.
